About TCBAMA Old Boys Association

Our Story

Founded in the early 1980s, TCBAMA Old Boys Association has become a powerful network of alumni dedicated to supporting our alma mater, fostering unity, and empowering future generations.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To unite alumni for mentorship, community development, and lifelong support to our alma mater.

Vision: To be a leading alumni association inspiring excellence and positive change globally.

Our Core Values

Unity

Bringing generations together in the spirit of brotherhood.

Excellence

Striving for success in all endeavors and inspiring others.

Service

Giving back to the community and alma mater through impactful projects.

Integrity

Operating with honesty, transparency, and strong ethics.

Our Journey

1983 - First Reunion

The association’s first major gathering of alumni.

1995 - Scholarship Program

Launched scholarships to support underprivileged students.

2010 - Infrastructure Projects

Contributed to building classrooms and facilities.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Created online platforms to connect alumni worldwide.
